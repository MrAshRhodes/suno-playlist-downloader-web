```bash
whois {domain} 2>/dev/null | grep -i "no match\|not found\|available\|DOMAIN NOT FOUND" | head -1
```

**3. Ranking table** — Rank all available domains by:
- **Memorability** (1-5): How easy to remember and type
- **Keyword SEO** (1-5): How well the domain signals "suno" + "download/music/mp3"
- **Brandability** (1-5): How professional and unique it sounds
- **TLD trust** (1-5): .com > .app > .io for general trust

**4. Top 3 recommendation** — Highlight the top 3 picks with brief rationale.
