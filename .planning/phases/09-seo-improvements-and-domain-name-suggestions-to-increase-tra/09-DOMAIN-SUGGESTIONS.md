## Top 3 Recommendations

### 1. sunozip.com -- BEST OVERALL

**Total score:** 20/20
**Estimated price:** ~$10-15/year (standard .com registration)

**Why this is the top pick:**
- **Perfect keyword match:** "suno" + "zip" describes the core feature (download Suno playlists as ZIP). Users searching for "suno zip download" or "suno playlist zip" will land here.
- **Maximum TLD trust:** .com is the default people type. No explanation needed. It works in every context -- social links, word of mouth, business cards.
- **Short and memorable:** 8 characters. Easy to spell, say aloud, and type from memory.
- **Brand differentiation:** No competitor uses "zip" in their domain. This is a unique angle in the Suno download space.
- **SEO alignment:** Exact-match keywords in domain still carry modest SEO weight. "sunozip" is novel enough to rank quickly.

### 2. getsuno.app -- BEST BRAND FEEL

**Total score:** 18/20
**Estimated price:** ~$14-20/year (.app registration)

**Why this is the runner-up:**
- **Action-oriented:** "Get Suno" is a call to action. It reads like a command -- go get your music.
- **Premium TLD:** .app is HTTPS-enforced by default (Google-operated), which signals modern and secure. Increasingly recognized for web tools.
- **Brand-first:** Less keyword-specific, more brand-friendly. Good if the product expands beyond ZIP downloads (e.g., adding MP3 preview, playlist management).
- **Memorable:** Two common English words. Easy to share verbally.
- **Slight SEO tradeoff:** Missing "download", "zip", or "mp3" keywords in the domain reduces keyword matching, but the .app TLD and strong brand compensate.

### 3. sunozip.app -- BEST HEDGE

**Total score:** 19/20
**Estimated price:** ~$14-20/year (.app registration)

**Why this is pick number three:**
- **Same keyword power as #1** but in the .app TLD, which is slightly less trusted by general audiences.
- **Register alongside sunozip.com:** Ideal strategy is to buy both sunozip.com and sunozip.app. Use .com as primary, .app as redirect. Prevents competitors from squatting the .app variant.
- **HTTPS-enforced:** .app requires HTTPS -- good security signal for users.
- **If sunozip.com gets taken first:** This is the immediate fallback with nearly identical strength.

### Recommended Registration Strategy

1. **Register sunozip.com immediately** -- this is the primary domain
2. **Also register sunozip.app** -- defensive registration + future redirect
3. **Consider getsuno.app** if planning to rebrand or expand product scope beyond ZIP downloads

## Next Steps

Once the user selects a domain:

1. **Register the domain** at a registrar (Namecheap, Cloudflare, Google Domains)
2. **Update canonical URL** in `client/src/index.html`: `<link rel="canonical" href="https://sunozip.com/" />`
3. **Update og:url** and all absolute URLs in OG/Twitter meta tags
4. **Update og:image URL** to `https://sunozip.com/assets/og-card.png`
5. **Update sitemap.xml** `<loc>` to the new domain
6. **Update robots.txt** Sitemap directive URL
7. **Update JSON-LD** `url` field
8. **Configure DNS** to point to Replit deployment (CNAME record)
9. **Set up HTTPS** (automatic with Replit custom domains or .app TLD)
10. **Verify in Google Search Console** with the new domain
